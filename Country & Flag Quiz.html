About Country & Flag Quiz Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Country & Flag Quiz Cyber-Edition! In an era of borderless hyper-networks, geographic knowledge remains a critical skill for any global netrunner.

How It Works

  • Data Retrieval: The system will intercept and display the official flag banner of a random Nation State from the legacy global database.
  • Identification: You will be provided with 4 possible terminal designations (Country Names). You must select the correct match.
  • Real-Time Feedback: The system instantly verifies your selection. Correct answers glow neon green. If you select incorrectly, the system flashes red and reveals the true answer.
  • Session Length: Each training simulation consists of 10 randomized flags drawn from a global pool of over 40 distinct regions.
  • Scoring: Achieve a perfect 10/10 to prove your mastery of old-world geographic data!
