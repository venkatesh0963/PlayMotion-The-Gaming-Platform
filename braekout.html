About Breakout Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Breakout Cyber-Edition! This reimagined version of the classic arcade game places you inside a mainframe under attack. Your mission: use your deflector shield to bounce the energy orb and smash through the security firewalls layer by layer.

How It Works

  • Movement: Use the Left / Right Arrow Keys or Mouse Movement to control your deflector shield along the bottom of the grid.
  • Scoring: Every time the energy orb shatters a firewall block, your score increases by 1. Keep the orb in play to break them all!
  • Game Over: The system will terminate your session if you let the energy orb fall past your deflector shield into the abyss. If you break all the blocks, you win and secure the mainframe!
  • Dynamic Themes: The game board dynamically updates based on your preferred system theme (Light or Dark mode). Use the toggle in the navigation bar to switch environments!

Whether you're looking for a quick retro gaming fix or aiming to top the Play Motion leaderboards, Breakout Cyber-Edition offers seamless, responsive gameplay powered entirely by modern HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.
