About Word Search Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Word Search Cyber-Edition! Dive deep into the raw data stream and extract the designated target keywords before the system resets.

How It Works

  • The Grid: A 12x12 matrix of randomized characters hides 8 specific cyberpunk-themed keywords.
  • Target Data: Check the "Data Targets" panel on the right. These are the strings you must locate within the grid.
  • Extraction: Click and drag your mouse across the letters to highlight a sequence. Words can be positioned horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. They can also be spelled backwards!
  • Verification: Releasing your mouse click verifies the sequence. If it matches a target keyword, the data is secured and will permanently glow neon green. It will also be crossed off your target list.
  • Objective: Find and isolate all 8 targets to achieve total system infiltration.

Stay sharp, hacker. The matrix is dense with garbage data, but the patterns are there if you look closely.
