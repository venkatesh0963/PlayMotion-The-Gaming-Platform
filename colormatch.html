About Color Match Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Color Match Cyber-Edition! Also known as SameGame or Collapse, this addictive puzzle tasks you with clearing a matrix of colored data blocks before you run out of moves.

How It Works

  • The Matrix: A 10x10 grid filled with randomly assigned neon data blocks.
  • Collapsing: Click on any group of 2 or more adjacent blocks of the same color. The entire connected cluster will collapse and disappear!
  • Gravity Dynamics: When blocks are removed, the blocks resting above them will instantly fall down to fill the void. If an entire vertical column is cleared, all remaining columns will shift to the left to close the gap.
  • Exponential Scoring: The more blocks you collapse in a single click, the exponentially higher your score will be. A cluster of 10 blocks is worth vastly more than five clusters of 2 blocks!
  • System Overload: The game ends when no more valid clusters of 2 or more blocks exist on the board. Try to clear the board entirely for a massive score bonus!

Calculate your moves, manipulate the gravity drops, and aim for massive combo chains!
