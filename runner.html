About Endless Cyber-Runner

Welcome to the Endless Runner Cyber-Edition! You are a rogue data packet sprinting across an infinite digital superhighway. Your objective is to dodge corrupted firewall blocks and survive as long as possible while the system steadily increases its processing speed.

How It Works

  • Movement: You run automatically. Press Spacebar, the Up Arrow Key, or simply Tap / Click the screen to jump over incoming obstacles.
  • Scoring: Your score represents the distance traveled. The longer you stay alive, the higher your score.
  • Escalating Difficulty: Be warned—as your distance increases, the speed of the digital grid accelerates, requiring faster reaction times!
  • Game Over: Colliding with any of the red corrupted firewall blocks will result in immediate system termination.
  • Dynamic Themes: The cyber-highway adapts to your preferred system theme (Light or Dark mode). Use the toggle in the navigation bar to switch environments!

Whether you're aiming for a quick reflex test or trying to dominate the Play Motion distance leaderboards, Endless Runner Cyber-Edition offers high-speed, responsive gameplay powered entirely by modern HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.
