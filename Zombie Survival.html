About Zombie Survival Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Zombie Survival Cyber-Edition! A virulent digital pathogen has infiltrated the core network, mutating dormant processes into hostile "zombie" malware protocols. You are the sole remaining antivirus entity.

How It Works

  • Movement: Utilize standard W, A, S, D navigation to evade the incoming swarms. Do not let them touch you.
  • Combat: Your avatar features 360-degree aiming capabilities. Track targets with your mouse cursor and click to discharge high-velocity plasma rounds.
  • Dynamic Threat Escalation: As time progresses, the system's "Threat Level" increases. This accelerates the spawn rate of hostile entities, overwhelming you from all borders of the matrix.
  • Particle Physics: Successfully neutralizing malware shatters their code into neon fragments, confirming data deletion.
  • Objective: Survive for as long as possible and purge as many infected programs as you can before they inevitably swarm your avatar and crash the system.

Keep moving, check your corners, and never stop firing. Good luck, Operator.
