About Platform Jumper Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Platform Jumper Cyber-Edition! You are a rogue data packet attempting to escape the corporate mainframe by leaping across fragmented server nodes.

How It Works

  • Auto-Jumping: Your avatar leaps automatically upon making contact with a solid platform surface. You only need to control horizontal movement.
  • Controls: Press Left / Right Arrow or A / D keys to navigate mid-air.
  • Screen Wrapping: The matrix wraps around seamlessly. If you jump off the left edge of the screen, you will instantly emerge on the right edge, and vice-versa. Use this to reach difficult platforms!
  • Ascension: As you climb higher, the camera pans upward and new platforms are procedurally generated above you. Your score increases relative to your maximum altitude achieved.
  • The Abyss: If you miss a platform and fall below the bottom edge of your terminal screen, your signal is lost permanently.

Keep a sharp eye on your trajectory, utilize screen wrapping, and climb as high as you can!
