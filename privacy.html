Data Extraction Protocols

LAST UPDATED: 2026.06.26 // PRIVACY POLICY

1. Zero-Knowledge Infrastructure

Welcome to the grid. Unlike the mega-corporations that harvest your telemetry, Play Motion operates on a strict zero-knowledge philosophy. We do not actively collect, store, or sell your personal biometric or digital hardware data. You are a ghost in our system.

2. Local Storage Variables

To ensure a persistent and optimized experience, our game modules may utilize your browser's localStorage API. This is exclusively used for:

  • Saving your preferred visual settings (e.g., toggling between Dark Neon and Light Cyber themes).
  • Storing high scores locally on your machine so you can track your personal bests without server-side databases.
  • Maintaining local game state variables between module reboots.

This data never leaves your terminal. It is not transmitted back to our central servers.

3. Absence of Tracking Pixels

We do not deploy third-party advertising algorithms, tracking cookies, or invisible pixels to monitor your navigation paths. Your traversal of the arcade remains encrypted by default through your standard HTTPS pipeline.

4. Subroutines and Error Logs

If the system encounters a fatal exception or logic loop, anonymous error telemetry may be generated by the host server strictly for debugging purposes. This data contains zero identifying markers and is automatically purged after the bugs have been isolated and patched.

5. External Links and Nodes

You may occasionally encounter links leading out of our controlled environment (e.g., social media portals). We do not control the data extraction protocols of external mega-corporations. Proceed into the broader web at your own risk.

6. Protocol Amendments

As the web evolves, so too must our security protocols. We reserve the right to patch this Privacy Policy as necessary. Any modifications will be logged directly on this page, keeping you informed of exactly how we treat your data.
