About Checkers Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Checkers Cyber-Edition! Command your cyan firewall nodes against the Crimson System AI in this classic strategy board game reinvented for the neon grid.

How It Works

  • Movement: Standard pieces move diagonally forward one square at a time. Click a piece to view available grid jumps.
  • Capturing: If an AI piece is adjacent to yours and the space behind it is empty, you can jump over it to capture and delete it from the grid.
  • King Promotion: If your piece reaches the far edge of the AI's territory, it gets upgraded to a King (★). Kings have the enhanced capability to move and capture diagonally backwards as well as forwards.
  • AI Opponent: The System AI automatically responds to your moves. Strategize to trap it and capture all of its pieces to win!
  • Dynamic Themes: The 8x8 grid adapts beautifully to Light or Dark mode via the navigation toggle.

Test your tactical foresight and dominate the board in this robust HTML5 Checkers experience.
