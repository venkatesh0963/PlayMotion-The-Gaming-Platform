About Sliding Puzzle Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Sliding Puzzle Cyber-Edition! The mainframe's core data blocks have been fragmented and scrambled. It is up to you to physically slide the blocks back into their correct sequential order.

How It Works

  • The Matrix: The puzzle consists of a 4x4 grid containing 15 numbered data blocks and one empty void slot.
  • Movement: Click on any block that is directly adjacent (up, down, left, right) to the empty void. The block will seamlessly slide into the empty space.
  • Tracking: Blocks that are currently in their correct final position will glow neon green to indicate a stable connection.
  • Objective: Rearrange the blocks sequentially so that 1 is in the top-left, 15 is adjacent to the bottom-right, and the empty void is exactly in the bottom-right corner.
  • Strategy: Plan your moves carefully! The system tracks your total move count. The fewer moves you make, the more efficient your hacking skills are.

Analyze the grid, slide the fragments, and execute the perfect data restoration sequence.
