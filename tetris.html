About Tetris Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Tetris Cyber-Edition! Dive into the ultimate digital stacking challenge. Falling neon data blocks (Tetrominoes) must be rotated and arranged to form complete horizontal lines without gaps.

How It Works

  • Clearing Data: When a complete horizontal line is formed, the data is purged, earning you points and freeing up space. If you clear 4 lines at once, you execute a "Tetris" for massive points!
  • Leveling Up: As you clear more lines, your Level increases. Each new level accelerates the speed at which the data blocks fall. Keep your reflexes sharp!
  • Ghost Piece: A translucent projection of your active block is cast at the bottom of the grid, allowing you to execute precision drops.
  • Hard Drop: Pressing the Spacebar instantly slams the block down, locking it into place. Useful for fast-paced, high-level play.
  • System Overflow: If the blocks stack all the way to the top of the grid and prevent new pieces from spawning, the system suffers a matrix overflow, resulting in a Game Over.

Stay calm, plan ahead by checking the "Next Fragment" display, and hack your way to the highest score!
