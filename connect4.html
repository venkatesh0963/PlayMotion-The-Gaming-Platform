About Connect Four Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Connect Four Cyber-Edition! Drop your neon data cores into the mainframe grid and outsmart the Crimson System AI in this classic alignment puzzle.

How It Works

  • Gameplay: Click or tap anywhere in a column to drop your cyan data core. It will fall to the lowest available slot.
  • Winning: The first player to align four of their cores in a row—either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—hacks the system and wins the round!
  • System AI: Be careful! The AI will actively try to block your horizontal, vertical, and diagonal setups while simultaneously building its own connections.
  • Dynamic Themes: The grid infrastructure adapts perfectly to your Light or Dark mode preferences via the navbar toggle.

Utilize gravity and strategy to dominate the grid in this beautifully crafted HTML5 interactive experience.
