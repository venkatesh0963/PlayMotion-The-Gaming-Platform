About Tic-Tac-Toe Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Tic-Tac-Toe Cyber-Edition! Dive into the simplest yet most strategic battle of wits on the digital grid. You are playing as 'X' against the rogue System AI ('O').

How It Works

  • Gameplay: Click or tap on any empty grid sector to claim it with your neon X. The AI will immediately counter with its crimson O.
  • Winning: Be the first to align three of your marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to hack the mainframe and win the round.
  • AI Logic: The System AI is programmed to block your winning moves and seize any opportunity to win itself. It won't go down without a fight!
  • Draw: If all 9 sectors are filled and neither side has aligned three marks, the game ends in a digital stalemate.
  • Dynamic Themes: The grid and your neon markers dynamically update based on your preferred system theme (Light or Dark mode).

Test your logic, trap the AI, and prove your superiority in this beautifully crafted HTML5 puzzle experience.
