About Tank Battle Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Tank Battle Cyber-Edition! Step into the role of an automated firewall defense construct tasked with eradicating hostile rogue tanks inside the corporate grid.

How It Works

  • Dual-Vector Controls: Master the tank controls by driving the hull with your keyboard (W, A, S, D) while simultaneously rotating your targeting turret independently with your mouse.
  • Combat Physics: Shells are lethal. Calculate your angles and use the neon grid walls for cover. Both your tank and the enemy tanks cannot pass through these hardcoded barriers.
  • Hostile AI: The rogue tanks are programmed to hunt you down. They will navigate the environment, track your movements, and rotate their turrets to fire upon you when you enter their line of sight.
  • Tactical Environment: Use the glowing data-blocks strategically. Hide behind them to reload, or lure enemy tanks into chokepoints.
  • Survival: A single shell impact results in critical structural failure. Destroy as many hostiles as possible before they tag you.

Keep your treads moving, watch your angles, and defend the grid.
