About Space Shooter

Welcome to the Space Shooter Cyber-Edition! You are piloting a state-of-the-art interceptor in the depths of the cyber-grid. Your mission is to defend the network from incoming waves of rogue data drones.

How It Works

  • Movement: Use the Left / Right Arrow Keys or Mouse Movement to maneuver your starfighter horizontally along the bottom axis.
  • Combat: Press the Spacebar or Left Click / Tap to unleash a barrage of neon lasers. Destroy incoming drones to score points!
  • Escalating Threats: The rogue drones will spawn faster and drop quicker as the game progresses, requiring sharp reflexes.
  • Game Over: The grid will collapse if an enemy drone collides with your ship, or if they breach your defenses and pass the bottom of the screen.
  • Dynamic Themes: The starfield dynamically updates based on your preferred system theme (Light or Dark mode).

Whether you're aiming for a quick arcade rush or trying to secure the highest score on Play Motion, Space Shooter Cyber-Edition offers frantic, responsive gameplay powered entirely by modern HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.
