About Pong Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Pong Cyber-Edition! This reimagined version of the foundational arcade game places you in a high-speed data rally against a relentless AI opponent. Deflect the data packet back and forth, aiming to outmaneuver the system and score.

How It Works

  • Movement: Use the Up / Down Arrow Keys or vertical Mouse Movement to control your paddle on the left side of the screen.
  • Scoring: Send the energy orb past the AI's paddle on the right side to score a point. If the orb slips past your paddle, the AI scores.
  • Game Over: The first player (or AI) to reach 5 points wins the match and secures the system.
  • Dynamic Themes: The arena dynamically updates based on your preferred system theme (Light or Dark mode). Use the toggle in the navigation bar to switch environments!

Whether you're looking for a quick retro gaming fix or aiming to top the Play Motion leaderboards, Pong Cyber-Edition offers seamless, responsive gameplay powered entirely by modern HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.
