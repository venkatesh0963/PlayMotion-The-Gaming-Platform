About Typing Speed Test Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Typing Speed Test Cyber-Edition! Prove your worth as an elite netrunner by executing raw data streams into the mainframe as fast as humanly possible.

How It Works

  • The Interface: Click "Initialize Interface" to generate a stream of cyberpunk lore and hacker terminology.
  • The Timer: The moment you type your first character, a 60-second countdown initiates. You must type continuously until the timer reaches zero.
  • Real-Time Feedback: The system validates every keystroke in real-time. Correctly inputted characters glow neon green, while corrupted data (typos) will flash warning crimson.
  • Metrics:
    • WPM (Words Per Minute): Calculated dynamically based on standard 5-character words. Only correctly typed characters contribute to this metric.
    • Accuracy: A percentage ratio of your valid keystrokes versus total keystrokes.
  • Progression: If you successfully type the entire paragraph before the timer ends, a fresh data stream will instantly generate so you can continue your run.

Keep your hands on the home row, maintain your accuracy, and see if you can break the 100 WPM barrier!
