About Data Slicer Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Data Slicer Cyber-Edition! Unprocessed data nodes are flooding the central repository. You are equipped with a high-frequency cursor laser capable of slicing through raw code.

How It Works

  • The Laser Blade: Hold Left-Click and drag your mouse across the canvas to draw a lethal energy trail. You must intersect the trajectory of the flying data nodes.
  • Data Nodes: Glowing geometric shapes will arc up from the bottom of the screen. Slicing them adds to your score and fragments their code into particles.
  • Logic Bombs: Watch out for the jagged, red, pulsing logic bombs. Slicing a bomb immediately crashes your system and results in a Game Over.
  • System Integrity: If a standard data node falls off the bottom of the screen without being sliced, you lose 1 unit of System Integrity (a heart). Lose all 3, and your session is terminated.
  • Combinations: Slice multiple nodes with a single continuous stroke for maximum efficiency.

Keep your blade sharp and your reflexes faster.
