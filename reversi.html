About Reversi Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Reversi Cyber-Edition! Dive into the classic strategy game of Othello, completely reimagined for the neon-drenched grid. Outflank the System AI and paint the digital landscape in your color.

How It Works

  • Objective: Have the majority of the data cores displaying your color (Cyan) when the board is completely filled.
  • Gameplay: On your turn, you must place a new core on an empty square. Your new core must "sandwich" at least one enemy Crimson core between itself and another of your existing Cyan cores, in any horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line.
  • Flipping: Any enemy cores caught in the sandwich are instantly converted (flipped) to your color!
  • Strategy: The System AI utilizes a greedy algorithm, constantly seeking moves that capture the most territory. Secure the corners and edges of the grid to protect your cores from being flipped!
  • Dynamic Themes: The grid interface adapts perfectly to Light or Dark mode preferences via the navbar toggle.
