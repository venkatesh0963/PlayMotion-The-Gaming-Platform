About Car Dodge Cyber-Edition

Welcome to the Car Dodge Cyber-Edition! You are a fast-moving packet navigating the central data superhighway. Unfortunately, the server is cluttered with corrupted ghost-data blocking the lanes.

How It Works

  • Controls: Press Left / Right Arrow or A / D keys to instantly shift your chassis between the three data lanes.
  • Velocity Scaling: As you travel further down the grid, the server overclock automatically engages. Your perceived speed will continuously increase, requiring faster reaction times.
  • Corrupt Packets: The red neon blocks representing corrupt data will stream downwards. If your chassis collides with any of them, your packet will be fragmented and the run terminates.
  • Scoring: Your score represents the total distance you have traveled without sustaining a critical collision. Survive as long as possible to top the leaderboards.

Keep your eyes on the horizon, anticipate the traffic, and don't crash.
